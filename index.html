Taco Town History

Tacos are becoming the new food truck trend! They are practically on any corner here in Indianapolis. There are many different ways you can make tacos. Some people use flour, some use corn, some fry them,and some don't. I think you get where we are going here, but We at Taco Town Market do things a little different! Everything can be a taco! We started building these items in our home back in 2015. We would always have cooking contest and you guessed it! Anything in a form a taco was the go too. We are known for our famous "Twinn Devil Taco"! Come shop with us to find ut more!
